How to get here

By plane
The nearest airport is Stuttgart Airport (Flughafen Stuttgart), located 34 km from Tübingen. Buses from the airport to Tübingen ("Airport Sprinter" X82 and 828) are available.

By train
Within Germany, you can travel by train to Tübingen via Stuttgart main station, which is connected to the European high-speed rail network and is served by both ICE trains of Deutsche Bahn and French TGV. From Stuttgart main station, local trains take about an hour to reach Tübingen and operate approx. every half an hour.
